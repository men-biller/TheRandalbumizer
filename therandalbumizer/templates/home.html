Welcome to The Randalbumizer

Discover new albums across multiple genres of music. Filter your preferences based on genre, years of release, and how many albums you desire. Hit generate for your randomized list and shuffle it for different options!

This website utilizes data taken from Album of the Year to generate random albums based on several filters and preferences. The database contains music from as far back as the 50s and as recently as 2024.
